Questions

Does it interpret my results?

Kinfolio organizes your records and shows each result against the reference range printed on your own report. It's an organizer, not a medical service — it does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Bring the clean picture to your healthcare professional; that conversation is the point.

What about different units between labs?

Unit conversion is one of the hardest problems in lab data and the reason naive trackers produce garbage trends. We normalize units carefully and preserve what the original document said, always.
