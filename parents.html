Questions

Is this a medical service?

No. Kinfolio is a personal health record organizer. It helps you keep, understand, and share your family's health information. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment — your healthcare professionals do that.

Can I manage more than one person?

Yes — that's the point. Separate profiles for each parent, your children, and yourself, all under one account you control.
