Questions

Which languages and labs are supported?

Documents in English, Arabic, and French from any laboratory — we extract from the document itself, so there's no dependence on portals or specific lab integrations.

Is this a medical service?

No. Kinfolio is a personal health record organizer. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment — it makes sure your real medical story travels with you to the professionals who do.
