How it works — three steps, zero integration

1

Send us the result PDF

The same PDF your machines already produce — email it, or forward it, or drop it in a shared folder. Any lab, any format, Arabic / English / French.

أرسل ملف النتيجة PDF كما يخرج من أجهزتك — لا حاجة لأي ربط تقني.

We generate the branded report

Our AI reads every marker, keeps your lab's own printed ranges and flags, and writes a plain-language bilingual summary — under your logo, in ~60 seconds.

Patient gets a WhatsApp link

They open a clean report they understand — the way they already receive results. They keep it, trend it over time, and come back to you.
